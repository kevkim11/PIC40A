About us

Hello! My name is Kevin Kim, CEO and head brewer of Kevin's Brewery, located in Eugene, OR. I was born in Korea but have lived in Eugene, OR throughout my childhood. Currently I am an undergraduate at UCLA majoring in Biochemistry and specializing in Computing. Using biochemical reactions using enzymes and computer programming, Kevin's Brewery offers a wide-selection of unique brews !

Using sophisticated computer programs, we have analyzed numerous proteins and have created a method in order to produce different synthetic enzymes to produce IPAs, lagers, and pale ale. Our signature beer, Kevin's signature, is brewed by our first developed enzyme, BEERASE.
